What is Disney?

Disney+ is the destination for the worlds of Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star, and more. A subscription to Disney+ gives you everything you can imagine and more: Find all the original productions as well as films, series, and short films from the worlds of Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star*, and more. Download as many movies and series as you want on up to 10 devices. Access movies and series in 4K UHD with Dolby Vision and Dolby Atmos on compatible devices at no extra cost. Discover GroupWatch, which allows you to watch any movie or series remotely with your family and friends via the Disney+ app.

How many devices can I use?

You can download movies and series to 10 devices at the same time, and you can watch your favorite content on 4 devices simultaneously.

Can I watch Disney+ on TV?

Yes! You can watch Disney+ on your TV via:

  • Smart TV
  • Streaming media players (Apple TV, Chromecast, Fire TV, Roku, etc.)
  • Consoles (PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S)
Can I create a Kids profile?

Yes, Disney+ offers the option to create a Kids profile, which allows your little ones to discover a selection of age-appropriate content without any risk of coming across adult content.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time directly in your Disney+ account settings. You will not be charged for the following month.
