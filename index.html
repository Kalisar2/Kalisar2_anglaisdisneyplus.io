Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney?

Disney+ is the destination for the worlds of Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star, and more.

What can I watch on Disney+?

Disney+ offers you a growing collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and films
  • Timeless classics and recent hits produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • More entertainment with comedies, dramas, fact-based stories, and much more content
  • Heartfelt stories for all ages brought to life by the talented artists at Pixar
  • The entire Star Wars saga and a large part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe
  • National Geographic documentaries, as well as special productions and much more

What is the cost of a Disney+ subscription?

Disney+ is commitment-free, and you can cancel your subscription whenever you want. The cancellation will take effect at the end of the period you have paid for. To unsubscribe, follow these 6 steps:

  • 1. Log in to www.DisneyPlus.com
  • 2. Select your profile
  • 3. Select Account
  • 4. Select your subscription
  • 5. Select Cancel Subscription
  • 6. Select Continue to cancel
  • 7. For more information, click here.
